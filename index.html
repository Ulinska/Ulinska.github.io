About me

Hello,

Hi, my name is Patrycja and I am a junior UX and UI designer.

If you're browsing my portfolio, you probably already know that I'm passionate about web design. So let's move on to something more interesting ...
What's different about me? My background maybe?
Up until now, I followed my passion for Asian culture and its aesthetics by earning a degree in Japanese Studies. I have finished my degree, but I continue to learn on my own.
The other thing is my obsession interest in video games and new technology. Name any Japanese game, and I've probably played it. Looking for a new gadget for your home, I've probably tested it.
Other than that, I am into ceramics and make sick(!) ceramic mugs in my spare time.
These three passions have merged into my love for nice looking and useful things - digital design.
That pretty much sums me up..
